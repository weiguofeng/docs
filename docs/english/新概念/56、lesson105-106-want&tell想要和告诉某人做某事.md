## 1、Question 

#### 	1、如何表达 想做某事？

​		**want to do sth == wanna do sth**

 		I want to buy a new car

​		Do you want to buy a new car？

#### 	2、如何表达 告诉某人做某事？

​		**tell sb to do sth**

​		Please tell him to bring me some coffee -- 请告诉他给我带一些咖啡





#### 	3、如何表达 犯错？
